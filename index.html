Welcome to High Serendip

Your Gateway to Sri Lanka's Wildlife Paradise

Located in the heart of Tissamaharama, High Serendip Safari Tours offers unforgettable wildlife experiences across Sri Lanka's most spectacular national parks. With expert guides, premium jeeps, and a passion for conservation, we bring you face-to-face with leopards, elephants, sloth bears, and countless bird species in their natural habitat.

Whether you're chasing the elusive leopard in Yala, exploring the serene wetlands of Bundala, or discovering the wilderness of Lunugamwehera, every safari with us is a journey into the wild heart of Sri Lanka.

Most Popular

Yala National Park

Yala National Park is a huge area of forest, in southeast Sri Lanka. Home to the world's highest density of leopards, Yala offers thrilling encounters with majestic big cats, elephants, and over 200 bird species.

  • Highest leopard density
  • Large elephant herds
  • 215+ bird species
View Safari Options

Lunugamwehera National Park

A pristine wilderness bordering Yala, offering peaceful safari experiences with diverse wildlife and stunning reservoir views.

  • Untouched wilderness
  • Rich biodiversity
  • Beautiful reservoir
View Safari Options

Bundala National Park

A wetland paradise and bird watcher's dream, designated as a Ramsar site with thousands of migratory birds and diverse ecosystems.

  • Flamingo sanctuary
  • Coastal wetlands
  • Crocodile habitats
