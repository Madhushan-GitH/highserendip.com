Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a safari?

The easiest way is via WhatsApp at +94 77 123 4567. You can also call us directly or use the contact form above. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance.

What's included in the safari price?

All safaris include park entrance fees, experienced guide and tracker, premium jeep, drinking water, and meals as specified (breakfast for morning safaris, lunch for full-day safaris).

What should I bring?

Comfortable clothing in neutral colors, hat, sunglasses, sunscreen, camera with zoom lens, and insect repellent. We provide water and meals.

Can you guarantee wildlife sightings?

While we can't guarantee specific animal sightings (they're wild!), our experienced guides know the best spots and times. Yala has excellent leopard sighting rates, and we consistently see elephants, deer, and various birds.

Do you provide hotel pickup?

Yes! We offer pickup from hotels in Tissamaharama and nearby areas. Please mention your hotel when booking, and we'll arrange pickup.
